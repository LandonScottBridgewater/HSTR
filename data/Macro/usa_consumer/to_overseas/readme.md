
Domestic = −36.5218+(0.0456×International)+(2.9302×t)

Example: Predict 2025

Year = 2025 → t = 2025 − 2010 = 15
t=2025−2010=15

If International = 2500 in 2025:
0.0456*2500=114.0
2.9302×15=43.953

Add to intercept:
Domestic=−36.5218+114.0+43.953≈121.43
